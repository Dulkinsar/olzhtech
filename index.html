Olzh Grid — Influence Mapping & Network Analytics

Reveal the invisible architecture of influence.

Olzh Grid is our graph-based analytics engine that transforms raw media signals, transaction logs, and internal datasets into dynamic network maps. Analysts can identify disruptors, trace influence paths, and backtrack narrative origins in real time.

Key Capabilities

  • Graph-Based Influence Engine (Neo4j-backed)
  • Centrality Mapping: Betweenness, Eigenvector, Impact Scores
  • Interactive Relationship Visuals w/ Zoom and Time Filters
  • Entity Resolution Across Multiple Data Streams
  • Narrative Traceback Engine for Propagation Mapping
  • Anomaly Overlay: Detect New or Suspicious Ties

Use Cases

  • Mapping disinformation campaigns in political ecosystems
  • Detecting coordinated influence networks across Telegram, VK, and forums
  • Identifying actors central to financial misinformation flows
  • Supporting crisis analysts in identifying destabilization attempts

SignalStack — Multi-Source OSINT Monitoring

Track narratives before they become threats.

SignalStack continuously monitors open-source environments — from Telegram and VK to forums, blogs, and public channels — to identify information spikes, sentiment shifts, and emerging threats. It transforms raw chatter into structured intelligence, in real time.

Key Capabilities

  • Real-Time Keyword & Entity Monitoring
  • Narrative Spike Detection & Alerting
  • Sentiment and Emotion Analysis via NLP
  • Geotagged Feed Aggregation (when available)
  • Multi-Source Format Support (text, video transcripts, metadata)
  • Optional Dark Web Plugin for pastebins, forums, and leak archives

Use Cases

  • Monitoring social media for early signs of unrest or crisis
  • Tracking foreign influence operations and bot activity
  • Alerting intelligence teams to narrative surges before virality
  • Identifying coordinated disinfo campaigns across multiple platforms
